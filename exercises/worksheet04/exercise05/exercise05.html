ANSWER for "Is Gouraud or Phong shading the best method for simulating highlight?":
  • Phong shading is more computationally expensive, but results in smoother highlight.
    • Gouraud Shading:
      • Lighting color is computed at each vertex, with intermediate fragment colors interpolated from these vertex colors. This can lead to less realistic lighting without a high vertex count.
    • Phong Shading:
      • Though more resource-intensive, Phong shading produces smoother highlights. Interpolating both positions and normals leads to more accurate inputs for shading calculations, enhancing realism.
