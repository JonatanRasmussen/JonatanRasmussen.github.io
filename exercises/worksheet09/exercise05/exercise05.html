  • Comparison of Projection Shadows and Shadow Mapping:
    • Projection Shadows:
      • Advantages:
        • Simple to implement and not demanding in terms of computation.
        • Cost-effective in terms of processing power.
      • Disadvantages:
        • Primarily effective for shadows on flat surfaces.
        • Limited options for altering the shadow's hue.
        • Unable to create shadows of the object onto itself.
    • Shadow Mapping:
      • Advantages:
        • Capable of rendering self-shadows.
        • Offers flexibility in merging the colors of the shadow and the shadowed object.
        • Suitable for objects of various complexities.
      • Disadvantages:
        • More complex to set up, requiring additional shaders and framebuffer objects.
        • Can be less efficient due to the need for an intermediate texture.
        • Prone to issues like aliasing and shimmering due to texture usage.
